Who It Is For

Talent is only the beginning.

We are looking for electronic artists with original work, a real desire to grow, and the willingness to follow through.

The strongest applicants will be curious, responsive, coachable, and capable of completing agreed-upon work without being chased.

What We Build

Identity

Positioning, artist narrative, biography, visual direction, and a clear point of view.

Infrastructure

Website, EPK, links, booking materials, mailing-list foundations, and reusable assets.

Execution

A practical roadmap connecting releases, performances, outreach, content, and long-term goals.

Participation Standard

We bring the framework. You do the work.

  • Attend scheduled sessions prepared and on time.
  • Complete agreed-upon work between sessions.
  • Communicate early when blocked or delayed.
  • Test strategies rather than rejecting them automatically.
  • Provide honest feedback and measurable outcomes.
  • Allow the process to become a public case study.

Participation may end early when an artist repeatedly misses sessions, avoids the work, or stops collaborating.
