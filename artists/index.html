Who It Is For

Talent is only the beginning.

A strong fit
  • Creates original electronic music
  • Wants a coherent long-term career
  • Responds, communicates, and follows through
  • Can accept useful feedback without collapsing or disappearing
  • Is willing to test ideas and measure outcomes
  • Builds their career from self-reliance, not dependency
Not a fit
  • Expects someone else to create the career
  • Needs constant chasing or motivation
  • Rejects strategy before trying it
  • Wants instant visibility without infrastructure
  • Is unwilling to document the process honestly
  • Expects others to carry the financial responsibilities of their career

Participation Standard

We bring the framework. You do the work.

  • 01

    Attend scheduled sessions prepared and on time.

  • 02

    Complete agreed-upon work between sessions.

  • 03

    Communicate early when blocked or delayed.

  • 04

    Test strategies before deciding they do not work.

  • 05

    Provide honest feedback and measurable outcomes.

  • 06

    Allow the process to become a public case study.

Participation may end early when an artist repeatedly misses sessions, avoids the work, or stops collaborating.
