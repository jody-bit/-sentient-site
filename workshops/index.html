Core Workshop

Building an Artist System.

This workshop examines the connected systems behind a sustainable electronic-music career: identity, narrative, booking readiness, digital infrastructure, relationships, and execution.

Participants identify weak links, clarify priorities, and leave with a practical framework for converting ambition into repeatable action.

Possible Topics

Practical, specific, adaptable.

  • Why talent is not enough.
  • Identity before algorithms.
  • Building a booking-ready artist presence.
  • EPKs that answer the promoter's real questions.
  • Turning releases into career momentum.
  • Collaboration, accountability, and follow-through.
