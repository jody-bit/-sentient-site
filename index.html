Founding Artists

Three artists. One working methodology.

Sentient will select three electronic artists for the first documented Artist Systems cohort.

Participants will receive strategy, positioning, narrative development, digital infrastructure, booking materials, and structured career support.

In exchange, participants must attend scheduled sessions, complete agreed-upon work, test the strategies, provide thoughtful feedback, and allow the process to be documented as a public case study.

This program is for artists ready to act, not simply collect advice.
